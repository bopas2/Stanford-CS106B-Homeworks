Secret Messages is the tenth studio album by Electric Light Orchestra (ELO), released in 1983 through Jet Records. 
It was the last ELO album with bass guitarist Kelly Groucutt, conductor Louis Clark, and real stringed instruments, and the last ELO album to be released on Jet Records. 
It was also the final ELO studio album to become a worldwide top 40 hit upon release.
